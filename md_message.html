Welcome to Global Medical Engineering (BD) Ltd.

“Global Medical Engineering (BD) Ltd” is the leading medical and health equipment supplier in Bangladesh. We are incorporated as a joint-stock company, established in 2007. Our mission is to provide the best service and widest the range of high-quality products in the health and medical sector. We serve our customers to every corner of the country, with the excellent world-renowned medical diagnostic reagents and other consumables, used in all fields of medical sectors.

We have achieved a great reputation for our high-quality, time & cost-saving products. Our company’s network is built across Bangladesh and the offices are equipped with high-end facilities and infrastructure for rendering services to the utmost satisfaction of our customers. With the help of our extensive sales and service network comprising of branches throughout the country and perpetually growing; we promise to offer individualized support to our clients; helping them to meet their daily challenges ever more efficiently.

Our highly skilled teams; trained both locally and internationally are spread across Bangladesh. They are exclusively operational and they are able to provide customers with immediate and hands-on technical support whenever required. “Global Medical Engineering (BD) Ltd” is one of the leading companies in the Medical & Laboratory Diagnostics industries of Bangladesh. Our customers include almost all Diagnostic Centers, Hospitals, Clinics, Medical Colleges, Research Centers and many more industries spread across Bangladesh.

Our vision is to maintain our position as the market leader in our fields of business and to expand our market to all corners of the country while fulfilling our environmental, social and economic responsibilities.
